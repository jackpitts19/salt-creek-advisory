Salt Creek Advisory LLC operates as an investment bank in the traditional lower middle market sense: we advise privately held business owners on the sale, acquisition, and capital-raising process for their companies. We are not a chartered bank or depository institution, we do not accept deposits, and no funds held in connection with an engagement are FDIC or NCUA insured. We are not a broker-dealer registered with FINRA or the SEC and are not a registered investment adviser. To the extent applicable, our M&A engagements are conducted in reliance on the M&A broker exemption at Section 15(b)(13) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. § 78o(b)(13)), under which “an M&A broker shall be exempt from registration” when acting solely in connection with the transfer of ownership of an “eligible privately held company,” defined as a company with no registered securities whose EBITDA is less than $25,000,000 or whose gross revenues are less than $250,000,000. That exemption is unavailable to any broker that holds client funds or securities, provides transaction financing, forms or represents a group of buyers, deals with a passive buyer, or represents both sides of a transaction without written disclosure and consent, and we do not engage in any of those activities. We hold or obtain state business broker licensure where required, and where a transaction calls for services only a licensed broker-dealer may perform, those services are referred to and executed through a properly registered broker-dealer. We do not hold, custody, or take possession of client funds or securities at any time. All engagements are advisory in nature; nothing on this site is an offer or solicitation to buy or sell any security, and nothing here is legal, tax, or investment advice. Consult your own qualified advisors.

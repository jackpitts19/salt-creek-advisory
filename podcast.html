Ep. 01 Where It Started

Gary Dennis

Gary Dennis left investment banking to build Mammoth Holdings into one of the country's leading car wash platforms, growing through 30-plus acquisitions and a partnership with the Pritzker Organization before stepping back as CEO after nearly 20 years. A first-generation kid from Macon, Georgia who treated car washes like manufacturing plants and memberships like a science.

Taylor's Candy

Doug Taylor, known as the Candyman, turned a small family operation into a full candy distribution and manufacturing business, picking up Windy City Popcorn along the way and steering through COVID with everything on the line. A conversation about building something real in an industry that runs entirely on relationships.

Salt Creek Advisory LLC operates as an investment bank in the traditional lower middle market sense: we advise privately held business owners on the sale, acquisition, and capital-raising process for their companies. We are not a chartered bank or depository institution, we do not accept deposits, and no funds held in connection with an engagement are FDIC or NCUA insured. We are not a broker-dealer registered with FINRA or the SEC and are not a registered investment adviser; to the extent applicable, our M&A engagements are conducted in reliance on the SEC’s M&A broker exemption and comparable state exemptions for business transfer intermediaries, and we hold or obtain state business broker licensure where required. We do not hold, custody, or take possession of client funds or securities at any time. All engagements are advisory in nature; nothing on this site is an offer or solicitation to buy or sell any security, and nothing here is legal, tax, or investment advice — consult your own qualified advisors. Where a transaction calls for services only a licensed broker-dealer may perform, those services are referred to and executed through a properly registered broker-dealer.

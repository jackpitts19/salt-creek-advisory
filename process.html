Our Process

A clear path from here
to what’s next.

No hand-offs. No shortcuts. The same two people from first call to close.

01

The First Call

One honest conversation about your business, your goals, and your timeline. No deck, no pitch. If we are not the right fit, we will say so and point you toward someone who is.

02

The Straight Answer

We dig into your numbers and value drivers, then give you an unfiltered read: what your business is likely worth, what would make it worth more, and whether now is even the right time. Sometimes the honest answer is "wait," and we will tell you that too.

03

The Game Plan

Selling or acquiring, we map the buyers worth calling: PE platforms, strategics, and family offices, vetted by hand. Then we build the materials that make them compete. Our target is first offers within 30 days of launch.

04

The Close

We run the data room, coordinate diligence, field offers, and negotiate terms while you keep running your business. Most deals close in six to nine months, with Jack and Connor on every call from first conversation to wire.

Salt Creek Advisory LLC operates as an investment bank in the traditional lower middle market sense: we advise privately held business owners on the sale and acquisition of their companies. We are not a chartered bank or depository institution, we do not accept deposits, and no funds held in connection with an engagement are FDIC or NCUA insured. We are not a broker-dealer registered with FINRA or the SEC and are not a registered investment adviser. To the extent applicable, our M&A engagements are conducted in reliance on the M&A broker exemption at Section 15(b)(13) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. § 78o(b)(13)), under which “an M&A broker shall be exempt from registration” when acting solely in connection with the transfer of ownership of an “eligible privately held company,” defined as a company with no registered securities whose EBITDA is less than $25,000,000 or whose gross revenues are less than $250,000,000. That exemption is unavailable to any broker that holds client funds or securities, provides transaction financing, forms or represents a group of buyers, deals with a passive buyer, or represents both sides of a transaction without written disclosure and consent, and we do not engage in any of those activities. We hold or obtain state business broker licensure where required, and where a transaction calls for services only a licensed broker-dealer may perform, those services are referred to and executed through a properly registered broker-dealer. We do not hold, custody, or take possession of client funds or securities at any time. All engagements are advisory in nature; nothing on this site is an offer or solicitation to buy or sell any security, and nothing here is legal, tax, or investment advice. Consult your own qualified advisors.

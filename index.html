The Case for a Process

What is your business
really worth?

Private equity firms control trillions in capital and employ teams of analysts whose sole job is to acquire your business at the lowest possible price. Our job is to make sure they pay the right one.

50% Higher Offers What competition can add. When buyers know they have rivals, the first bid stops being the ceiling.
30 Days to First Offers Our target from launch to first offers. Speed creates leverage, and leverage creates value.
$0 Upfront Fees Success fee only. No retainers. We get paid when you do. That is the deal.
100% Aligned Incentives Every buyer that matters. None that do not. The right table, not the biggest one.
Buyers do not overpay in private conversations.
They overpay when they are competing.

We build a structured, competitive process around your business and your timeline. Every relevant buyer at the table, at the same time, with the same information. That is how you get a number that actually reflects what you built. Not what a buyer thought they could get away with in a private conversation.

Why Salt Creek

A different kind
of investment bank.

Buy-side and sell-side M&A is all we do. We built a model that is more direct, more aligned, and more owner-friendly than what most banks offer. Here is what that looks like in practice.

01

You Deal With Us Directly

Jack and Connor work every engagement personally, from first call to close. You always know who is on your deal and who is accountable for the outcome. That is not how most banks operate, and it is the difference that matters most.

02

When You Win, We Win

Salt Creek is 100% success-fee based. We only get paid when your deal closes. That means our incentives are completely aligned with yours. Not just getting a deal done, but getting you the right one.

03

We Cut the Complexity

Traditional investment banking is full of unnecessary process. Long timelines, binders nobody reads, jargon that obscures more than it explains. We keep things clear. You always know where things stand and why.

04

Proprietary AI

We built our own AI tools for research, deal preparation, and market intelligence. Most boutique advisors haven't come close. The result is faster, sharper work that gives you a real edge in the process.

A real process gets you
a better deal.

Most owners only sell once, and many take the first offer they get. The difference between a good outcome and a great one is almost always whether there was a real process, one where multiple buyers compete. When buyers know they are not the only option, they sharpen their offers. Price goes up. Terms improve. You have leverage. Running a competitive process is one of the most important things a good advisor does, and it is exactly what we do.
